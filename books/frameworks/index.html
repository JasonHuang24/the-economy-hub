Volume I

Frameworks

Economic systems, in theory and in practice.

Available

Frameworks takes up the systems by which societies organize production and exchange: markets, plans, commons, and the mixtures that govern nearly everyone alive. Each chapter states what a system claims about itself, then follows what it does in practice.

Every chapter answers a single question, and each concept is treated here once; the other volumes point back rather than restate.
