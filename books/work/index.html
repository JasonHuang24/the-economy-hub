Volume IV

Work

Labor, hours, wages, and the purpose of a job.

Forthcoming

Work asks what a job is worth and why it pays what it does: what makes work good or bad, why the paycheck loses the race with a growing economy, and the charged questions that follow, about the minimum wage, the pay gap, immigration, executive pay, care, hours, machines, and what a job is finally for. Each chapter takes one question and follows the evidence to an answer.

The first two chapters are available below; the rest are in preparation and will be released as the volume is written. Every concept is treated here once, and the other volumes point back rather than restate.
